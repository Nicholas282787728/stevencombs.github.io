--- layout: post title: MSSC for active-duty military date: 2012-07-11 05:30:03 UTC updated: 2012-07-11 05:30:03 UTC comments: false categories: certification higher-education military --- I have a personal interest in this topic as an Ivy Tech academic officer and a retired military service member. Inside Indiana has just posted that the Ivy Tech Corporate College (the non-credit side of the college) will offer the Manufacturing Skill Standards Certification to active-duty military who are soon to begin their transition from military to civilian.

The certification will assist in the acquisition of jobs in manufacturing and logistics. Read the entire story here to learn more and to find out which campus locations will offer this program. I am happy to report that our campus is on this list.
