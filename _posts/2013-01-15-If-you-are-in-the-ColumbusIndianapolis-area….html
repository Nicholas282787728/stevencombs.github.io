Ivy Tech hosts “An evening of Comedy IMPROV” 
Ivy Tech Community College – Columbus/Franklin invites the public to “An evening of Comedy IMPROV” from 7:00 until 10:00 p.m. on Friday, January 25. This event, to be held at The Commons, will be an evening of improvisational comedy – fast-paced entertainment that’s fun for everyone, 21 and over. With audience participation and suggestions, two teams from ComedySportz Indianapolis will battle for laughs as they make up scenes and songs on the spot.

Food and beverages will be provided by 450 North Brewing Company/Simmons Winery. A cash bar will also be available.

Sponsors include platinum sponsor The Republic, gold sponsors Faurecia and White River Broadcasting, silver sponsors Centra Credit Union and Taylor Brothers, bronze sponsors 450 North Brewing Company/Simmons Winery, LHP, and Old National Bank.

Tickets prices are $45 for one and $80 for a couple and may be purchased online at www.ivytechimprov.com or by calling 812-374-5186.
